What are Protected Areas?

According to the International Union for Conservation of Nature (IUCN), Protected Areas are clearly defined geographical space, recognized, dedicated, and managed, through legal or other effective means, to achieve the long-term conservation of nature with associated ecosystem services and cultural values.


IUCN

Sets global standards to improve the effectiveness of protected areas while promoting justice, equity, and the rights of Indigenous peoples and local communities

Protected Areas Categories

  • 🍃 Ia: Strict Nature Reserve
  • 🍃 Ib: Wilderness Area
  • 🍃 II: National Park
  • 🍃 III: Natural Monument or Feature
  • 🍃 IV: Habitat/Species Management Area
  • 🍃 V: Protected Areas with Sustainable Use of Natural Resources

Why Protected Areas are Important

  • 🍃 Foundation of biodiversity conservation
  • 🍃 Safeguard nature and culture resources
  • 🍃 Improve livelihoods
  • 🍃 Drive Sustainable development
